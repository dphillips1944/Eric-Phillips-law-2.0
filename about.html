Who We Are

At Eric F. Phillips & Associates, we understand that facing legal challenges can be overwhelming and deeply personal. Whether you are dealing with criminal defense, family law, bankruptcy, stalking and protection orders, or wills and trusts, our team is here to support you every step of the way. We take the time to listen, understand your situation, and guide you toward the best possible outcome.

Meet Eric F. Phillips

Eric F. Phillips has been practicing law since 2004 and has represented clients through some of the most difficult moments of their lives. Born and raised in Wyoming, Eric is deeply connected to the community he serves and earned his Juris Doctor from the University of Wyoming in 2004.

As a former prosecutor and County Attorney who transitioned to defense work more than a decade ago, Eric brings a unique perspective and a deep understanding of how to guide clients through the legal system with care and clarity.

Eric is known for being approachable, compassionate, and easy to talk to. He takes pride in helping clients feel at ease during stressful and uncertain times while remaining focused on the best possible outcome.

Meet Elizabeth "Beth" Lempp-Fields

Beth has been practicing law since 2019 and focuses almost exclusively on family law, including divorce and child custody. She is a graduate of the University of Wyoming College of Law and has dedicated her practice to helping clients through emotionally difficult and highly personal legal matters.

She is known as a determined advocate who is fully prepared to take cases to trial when needed, while also being a strong negotiator who resolves many cases through settlement.

Beth approaches each case with both strength and compassion, working to protect what matters most to her clients while helping them move toward stability.
